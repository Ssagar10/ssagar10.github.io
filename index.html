Superheroes

  • Superman
  • Spiderman
  • Batman

This paragraph describes about my three favorite superheroes. Born on the planet Krypton, Superman was raised on Earth and uses his abilities to protect humanity from various threats. Spider-Man, or Peter Parker, gained his powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider, granting him incredible agility and the ability to stick to walls. Batman, or Bruce Wayne, is a vigilante who uses his vast wealth, intellect, and physical prowess to fight crime in Gotham Cit.

All of the superheroes mentioned above had one thing in common, to do good for humanity.
